Privacy Policy for The Aesthetic Realm

Use of Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience. Cookies are small text files
stored on your device that allow us to track site usage and tailor content to your preferences.

We use third-party vendors, including Google, to display ads on our website.
These vendors may use cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies
to collect information about your interactions with ads and websites across the internet.

Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google’s use
of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads based on
your prior visits to our site and other sites on the internet.

You can learn more about how Google uses data by visiting
Google's Privacy & Terms page. If you wish to opt-out of personalized advertising,
you can do so by visiting Google Ad Settings or the
Network Advertising Initiative Opt-Out Page.

User Consent

By using our website, you consent to our privacy policy and the
collection and use of your data as described herein, including the
use of cookies and data collection by third parties, such as Google.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.
