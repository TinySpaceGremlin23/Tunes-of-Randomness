tinyspacegremlin

Short Version: This is a music player.
It's a player that plays music.

Long Version: I made a random music player that includes
all the videos I have saved in a playlist on my YouTube channel. I
downloaded the videos, converted them in mp3 files, and then
shoved them in a JSON file for the player to pull from. These are
videos I've saved over the years (my playlist currently says 3,670),
and even though some might have gotten lost in process,
I still wanted to share my music with everyone! If there's
a song you want to hear, and if it isn't already in the list, the
watermark in the lower right corner will take you straight to
my Instagram, where you can DM me your request.
